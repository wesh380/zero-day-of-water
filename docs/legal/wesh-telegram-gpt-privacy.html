Privacy Policy for Wesh Telegram Publisher GPT

Last updated: 2025-10-08

This page explains how data is processed when using this GPT to publish to a Telegram channel.

  • What we collect: The text you input (and optional photo URLs/captions) to be sent to Telegram.
  • How we use it: We call the Telegram Bot API to post the content to your channel. No other use.
  • Sharing: Telegram receives the content necessary to deliver the message. We do not share data elsewhere.
  • Retention: We do not persist messages. Minimal transient state (e.g., last published IDs) may be kept for reliability.
  • Your rights: Stop using the GPT any time; on request we can delete any retained operational state.

Contact: info@wesh360.ir

This policy applies to the custom GPT and complements OpenAI’s data policies.
